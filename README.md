# killer.com
Fire The Gun is a high-intensity, action-packed shooting experience built for players who want speed, skill, and strategy in one battlefield. Step into a realistic combat arena where every move matters and every shot counts.

From intense 1v1 duels to challenging bot battles, the game delivers a dynamic environment where players must survive, adapt, and dominate. With smooth controls, modern visuals, and immersive gameplay, Fire The Gun brings the thrill of competitive shooting directly to your screen.
